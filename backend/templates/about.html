About

How this project works

Distributed ESP32 nodes collect air and water data around Turkey Point. Readings are sent to the server over HTTPS and interpreted with a lightweight AI layer to highlight unusual patterns. Data is stored for transparency and trend analysis.

  • Modular nodes with 3D-printed enclosures
  • Ground sensors: radiation, PM2.5, temperature, humidity, pressure, VOC
  • Water sensors: temperature, turbidity, TDS, pH
  • Server stores readings and computes status updates

This system does not replace official safety systems. It provides an additional layer of public transparency.
